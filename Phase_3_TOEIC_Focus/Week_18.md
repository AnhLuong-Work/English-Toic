## TOEIC Part 7 — Speed Reading Mastery

### Chiến thuật theo loại văn bản

**Email & Thư từ:**
- Từ khóa: From/To/Subject → xác định người gửi, nhận, chủ đề
- Đọc câu đầu và câu cuối mỗi đoạn
- Chú ý: dates, numbers, requests, deadlines

**Thông báo & Quảng cáo:**
- Đọc tiêu đề và bullet points trước
- Tìm: ai được hưởng lợi, điều kiện, thời hạn

**Hướng dẫn & Quy trình:**
- Đọc theo thứ tự bước
- Chú ý: "must", "required", "prohibited"

**Báo cáo & Bài báo:**
- Đọc đoạn đầu (abstract/summary) và đoạn cuối
- Tìm con số, xu hướng

### Luyện tập Part 7 — Bộ 3 văn bản (Triple Passage)

**Văn bản 1 — Email từ nhà cung cấp:**

> From: Robert Kim, Sales Manager — Pacific Supplies  
> To: Jennifer Wong, Procurement Manager — TechViet Corp  
> Date: August 10, 2026  
> Subject: Contract Renewal Proposal
>
> Dear Ms. Wong,
>
> Further to our conversation last week, I am pleased to submit our contract renewal proposal for the 2027 fiscal year. As per our current agreement, we have maintained a 99.2% on-time delivery rate and zero defects over the past 12 months.
>
> We are proposing a 3-year renewal with the following terms:
> - Unit pricing: reduced by 5% from current rates
> - Minimum order quantity: 500 units per month
> - Payment terms: Net 30
> - Delivery: within 5 business days of order confirmation
>
> Please find attached the detailed proposal document. We look forward to continuing our partnership with TechViet. Should you require any clarification, please do not hesitate to contact me.
>
> Best regards,  
> Robert Kim

**Văn bản 2 — Email nội bộ:**

> From: Jennifer Wong  
> To: David Chen, Finance Director  
> Subject: FW: Contract Renewal Proposal — Pacific Supplies
>
> Hi David,
>
> Please review the attached proposal from Pacific Supplies. Their performance has been excellent this year. The 5% price reduction is attractive, but I'm concerned about the 3-year commitment and the minimum order quantity.
>
> Can we schedule a meeting this week to discuss? I'd also appreciate your assessment of whether the Net 30 terms align with our current cash flow situation.
>
> Jennifer

**Văn bản 3 — Biên bản cuộc họp:**

> **Meeting Minutes — Procurement Review**  
> Date: August 12, 2026 | Attendees: J. Wong, D. Chen, L. Park (Legal)
>
> Discussion Points:
> 1. Pacific Supplies proposal reviewed — pricing favorable
> 2. Legal team flagged that the 3-year term requires board approval per company policy
> 3. Finance confirmed Net 30 terms are acceptable
> 4. Minimum order of 500 units aligned with current production needs
>
> Decision: Counter-propose a 2-year agreement with an option to extend. Board approval required before signing. Jennifer to respond to Pacific Supplies by August 20th.

**Câu hỏi:**
1. What is Robert Kim's reason for writing?
   (A) To report a delivery delay  (B) To propose a contract renewal  (C) To request a meeting  (D) To complain about payment

2. What concern does Jennifer express about the proposal?
   (A) The price reduction is insufficient  (B) The delivery time is too long  (C) The 3-year commitment and order quantity  (D) The payment terms

3. According to the meeting minutes, what is required before signing?
   (A) Jennifer's approval  (B) Finance director approval  (C) Board approval  (D) Legal team approval

4. What is Jennifer's deadline to respond to Pacific Supplies?
   (A) August 10  (B) August 12  (C) August 15  (D) August 20

5. What counter-proposal was decided?
   (A) 1-year agreement  (B) 2-year with extension option  (C) 3-year with price increase  (D) 5-year partnership

*Đáp án: 1-B, 2-C, 3-C, 4-D, 5-B*
