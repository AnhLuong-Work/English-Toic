## TOEIC Part 6 — Mastery

### Chiến thuật toàn diện

**4 loại câu hỏi trong Part 6:**
1. **Từ loại** (giống Part 5) — xác định vị trí, chọn noun/adj/adv/verb
2. **Thì động từ** — nhìn vào thời gian trong đoạn, tìm clues (last week, currently, next month)
3. **Từ nối/Liên từ** — đọc câu trước và sau, xác định quan hệ logic
4. **Chèn câu** — đọc câu trước + sau, tìm từ nối, đại từ, logic

### Ví dụ Part 6 đầy đủ

> **Memorandum**
>
> To: All Department Heads  
> From: Operations Director  
> Subject: Office Renovation Update
>
> As you may be aware, the renovation of our main office building ______(1)______ next month. During this period, staff from the Finance and HR departments will ______(2)______ to the third floor of Building B.
>
> ______(3)______. Employees who require access to equipment or files during the renovation should make arrangements with their respective managers at least one week in advance.
>
> We ______(4)______ that the renovation will cause minimal disruption. If you have any concerns or questions, please do not hesitate to contact the Facilities Management team.

**Câu hỏi:**

1. (A) begin  (B) began  (C) will begin  (D) has begun

2. (A) relocate  (B) be relocated  (C) relocating  (D) relocated

3. (A) The renovation project was completed ahead of schedule.
   (B) All office furniture will be removed and stored temporarily.
   (C) We apologize for the inconvenience this may cause.
   (D) The main office building was recently expanded.

4. (A) ensure  (B) assure  (C) insure  (D) B only

*Đáp án: 1-C (next month = future), 2-B (passive — staff will be moved), 3-B (logical continuation about what happens during renovation), 4-B (assure = cam đoan với người — "we assure you that...")*
