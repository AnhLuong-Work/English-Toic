**Bài tập — Điền collocation đúng:**
1. The company ______ a survey to understand employee satisfaction.
2. New regulations will ______ from January 1st.
3. All staff are encouraged to ______ the training program.
4. Please ______ a note of the key discussion points during the meeting.

*Đáp án: 1-did/conducted, 2-take effect, 3-take part in, 4-take, 5-give/obtain*

## TOEIC Part 4 — Mastery

### Nhận biết loại bài trong 3 giây đầu

| Từ đầu tiên | Loại bài |
|------------|---------|
| "Attention..." | Thông báo tại chỗ (store/office) |
| "Thank you for calling..." | Thư thoại tự động |
| "Good morning/afternoon, everyone..." | Cuộc họp/Hội thảo |
| "Are you looking for...?" | Quảng cáo |
| "We'd like to inform you..." | Thông báo công ty |
| "Passengers are advised..." | Thông báo sân bay/ga tàu |
| "The weather for today..." | Bản tin thời tiết |
| "In today's news..." | Bản tin |
| "If you have any questions, press..." | Hệ thống điện thoại tự động |

### 3 câu hỏi thường gặp và cách trả lời

**Câu 1 - "What is the purpose of this announcement?"**
→ Nghe câu đầu tiên + 2 câu sau → xác định mục đích chính

**Câu 2 - "Who is the intended audience?"**
→ Nhận biết qua từ xưng hô: "all employees", "valued customers", "passengers"

**Câu 3 - "What are listeners asked to do?"**
→ Nghe câu cuối — thường có: "please call", "visit", "submit", "contact"

### 3 bài Part 4 thực chiến

**Bài 1:**
> "Good afternoon, shoppers. We'd like to draw your attention to today's special promotion. For the next two hours only, all items in our electronics department are 30% off. This includes televisions, laptops, and smartphones. Take advantage of this limited-time offer and make your way to the second floor. Our knowledgeable staff are available to assist you. This offer ends at 5pm today."

Q1: Where is this announcement being made?
Q2: What is on sale?
Q3: How long does the promotion last?

*Đáp án: 1-A store/shopping mall, 2-Electronics (TVs, laptops, smartphones), 3-2 hours*

**Bài 2:**
> "You have reached TechViet's customer service line. Our offices are currently closed. Our business hours are Monday through Friday, 8am to 6pm. If you are calling about a technical issue, please press 1. For billing inquiries, press 2. To leave a message for our team, please stay on the line. We will return your call within one business day. Thank you for choosing TechViet."

Q1: Why can't the caller reach anyone?
Q2: What should someone do for billing questions?
Q3: When will the call be returned?

*Đáp án: 1-Office is closed, 2-Press 2, 3-Within one business day*

**5 câu Part 5 — Collocation:**
1. The board ______ a decision to postpone the product launch.
   (A) did  (B) made  (C) had  (D) took

2. The new environmental policy will ______ effect on April 1st.
   (A) come into  (B) take  (C) go into  (D) B and C

3. We need to ______ action before the deadline passes.
   (A) make  (B) give  (C) do  (D) take

4. The CEO will ______ a speech at the annual conference.
   (A) make  (B) give  (C) do  (D) take

5. All employees are invited to ______ part in the company survey.
   (A) make  (B) have  (C) take  (D) give

*Đáp án: 1-B, 2-D, 3-D, 4-B, 5-C*
