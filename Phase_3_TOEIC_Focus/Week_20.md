## Ngữ pháp — Ôn Tổng Hợp (Những điểm hay sai nhất)

### Subjunctive Mood (Cầu thỉnh cách)
Dùng sau: suggest, recommend, require, request, propose, insist, demand

**Công thức: + that + S + V (nguyên thể, không chia)**
- The manager **suggested** that the team **submit** the report early.
- It is essential **that** all employees **be** notified.
- The board **required** that the decision **be** made unanimously.

**Bài tập:**
1. The CEO proposed that the merger (be/was/is) approved by the end of Q3.
2. It is crucial that each department (submits/submit/submitted) its budget.
3. The regulations require that all contracts (are/be/being) reviewed by legal.

*Đáp án: 1-be, 2-submit, 3-be*

### Câu điều kiện loại 3 (Unreal Past)
Diễn đạt điều trái với thực tế trong quá khứ:
```
If + S + had + V3, S + would/could/might + have + V3
```
- If we **had hired** more staff, we **would have met** the deadline.
- If the patent **had been filed** earlier, the litigation **might not have occurred**.

**Bài tập:**
1. If the board ______ (approve) the budget earlier, the project ______ (not/be delayed).
2. If they ______ (conduct) due diligence, they ______ (discover) the issue.

*Đáp án: 1-had approved / would not have been delayed, 2-had conducted / would have discovered*
