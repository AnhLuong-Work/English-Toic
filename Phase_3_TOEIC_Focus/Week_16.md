## Suffix Nhận Biết Từ Loại Nhanh

### Danh từ (Noun) — Hậu tố
| Hậu tố | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| -tion / -sion | information, decision, presentation |
| -ment | management, improvement, payment |
| -ness | effectiveness, awareness, weakness |
| -ity / -ty | productivity, responsibility, quality |
| -ance / -ence | performance, compliance, conference |
| -er / -or / -ist | manager, supervisor, specialist |
| -ship | leadership, partnership, membership |
| -age | coverage, percentage, storage |

### Tính từ (Adjective) — Hậu tố
| Hậu tố | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| -ive | effective, productive, comprehensive |
| -al / -ial | financial, annual, managerial |
| -able / -ible | available, responsible, flexible |
| -ous | previous, numerous, various |
| -ful | successful, thoughtful, useful |
| -less | useless, endless, seamless |
| -ary / -ory | preliminary, mandatory, satisfactory |
| -ent / -ant | efficient, relevant, significant |

### Trạng từ (Adverb) — Hậu tố
| Hậu tố | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| -ly | efficiently, significantly, promptly |
| -ward(s) | forward, outward, upward |
| -wise | otherwise, likewise, clockwise |

### Động từ (Verb) — Hậu tố
| Hậu tố | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| -ize / -ise | organize, prioritize, customize |
| -fy / -ify | notify, clarify, identify |
| -en | strengthen, broaden, heighten |
| -ate | communicate, negotiate, evaluate |

## Part 5 Speed Training — 30 Câu (10 phút)

**BẮT GIỜ! Làm 30 câu này trong 10 phút:**

1. The HR department sent a ______ to all staff about the new benefits package.
   (A) notify  (B) notified  (C) notification  (D) notably

2. The new CEO is known for her ______ and innovative management style.
   (A) effective  (B) effectiveness  (C) effect  (D) effectively

3. All employees are required to ______ with the company's code of conduct.
   (A) compliant  (B) compliance  (C) comply  (D) complying

4. The project was completed ______ and within budget.
   (A) success  (B) successful  (C) successfully  (D) succeed

5. The committee has ______ three candidates for the position.
   (A) identification  (B) identify  (C) identified  (D) identifying

6. A ______ assessment of the risks is required before proceeding.
   (A) comprehend  (B) comprehension  (C) comprehensive  (D) comprehensively

7. The finance team must ______ all expense reports by the end of the month.
   (A) authorize  (B) authorization  (C) authorized  (D) authorizing

8. Sales figures ______ significantly during the peak season.
   (A) rise  (B) arose  (C) increased  (D) risen

9. The new regulation will take ______ at the beginning of next quarter.
   (A) affect  (B) effect  (C) effectively  (D) effective

10. We need someone who can ______ complex technical information clearly.
    (A) communication  (B) communicate  (C) communicative  (D) communicatively

11. The ______ of the board's decision was communicated via email.
    (A) rational  (B) rationale  (C) rationalize  (D) rationally

12. Staff members are encouraged to ______ their skills through ongoing training.
    (A) strengthen  (B) strength  (C) strong  (D) strongly

13. The company's ______ to customer satisfaction is evident in their service.
    (A) commit  (B) committed  (C) commitment  (D) commitments

14. Please ______ the attached document and sign where indicated.
    (A) review  (B) reviewer  (C) reviewed  (D) reviewing

15. The team has ______ worked together on this type of project before.
    (A) previous  (B) previously  (C) preview  (D) prevision

16. ______ the challenging market conditions, the company maintained profitability.
    (A) While  (B) Despite  (C) Although  (D) Because

17. The new policy ______ be effective starting January 1st.
    (A) is  (B) will  (C) has  (D) does

18. Only after all approvals ______ received can the project begin.
    (A) have been  (B) was  (C) are being  (D) be

19. The results of the evaluation ______ to all department heads last week.
    (A) was distributed  (B) were distributed  (C) distributed  (D) distributing

20. ______ submitted by the deadline, applications will not be considered.
    (A) Unless  (B) Until  (C) If not  (D) Unless not

21. The merger agreement ______ three months of intense negotiations.
    (A) followed  (B) resulted  (C) required  (D) preceded

22. The sales team ______ to reach their quarterly target by October.
    (A) is expected  (B) expects  (C) expected  (D) are expecting

23. The manager asked that all reports ______ submitted digitally.
    (A) are  (B) be  (C) were  (D) have been

24. The new software is not only user-friendly ______ also highly efficient.
    (A) but  (B) and  (C) or  (D) yet

25. ______ the CEO's approval, the project cannot move forward.
    (A) Lacking  (B) Without  (C) Despite  (D) Unless

26. The seminar ______ by over 200 participants from across the industry.
    (A) attended  (B) was attended  (C) has attended  (D) attending

27. The company is ______ to providing equal opportunities for all employees.
    (A) commit  (B) committed  (C) committing  (D) commitment

28. The annual report will be ______ to all shareholders next Friday.
    (A) distributed  (B) distributing  (C) distribute  (D) distribution

29. ______ of the proposed changes were approved by the board.
    (A) All  (B) Every  (C) Each  (D) Any

30. Sales ______ by 25% compared to the same period last year.
    (A) was grown  (B) grew  (C) has grown  (D) B and C are both correct
