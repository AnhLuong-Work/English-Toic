### Ngữ pháp — So sánh kép & Quantifiers

#### So sánh kép (Double Comparatives)
Ý nghĩa: "Càng A thì càng B"
```
The + comparative, the + comparative
```
- **The more** you invest, **the higher** the returns.
- **The faster** the cash flow improves, **the stronger** the company becomes.
- **The fewer** the overhead costs, **the higher** the net profit.

#### Quantifiers (Từ chỉ số lượng)

| Quantifier | Dùng với | Ý nghĩa |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| **many** | Đếm được | many stakeholders |
| **much** | Không đếm được | much revenue |
| **a few** | Đếm được (một vài = có) | a few investors |
| **few** | Đếm được (rất ít = tiêu cực) | few options |
| **a little** | Không đếm được (một chút = có) | a little leverage |
| **little** | Không đếm được (rất ít = tiêu cực) | little cash flow |
| **a lot of / lots of** | Cả hai | a lot of profit |
| **several** | Đếm được | several quarters |

**Bài tập:**
1. The (more/most) we reduce overhead, the (higher/highest) our net profit.
2. There is (few/little) cash flow available this quarter.
3. (Many/Much) stakeholders were concerned about the acquisition.
4. We have (a few/a little) options for the merger.
5. The (further/more far) we expand, the (greater/great) the risk.

*Đáp án: 1-more/higher, 2-little, 3-Many, 4-a few, 5-further/greater*

## Ngày 3–4 (Thứ Tư–Thứ Năm) — TOEIC Part 1 Nâng Cao

### Mô tả hình ảnh — từ vựng chuyên biệt

**Môi trường văn phòng:**
- A man/woman is **seated at** a desk
- Documents are **stacked/piled** on the table
- Equipment is **arranged/displayed** on shelves
- A computer **screen** is showing data

**Môi trường sản xuất/kho:**
- Workers are **operating** machinery
- Goods are **being loaded onto** a truck
- Items are **stacked in** the warehouse
- A forklift is **transporting** cargo

**Môi trường ngoài trời/công cộng:**
- People are **walking along** the corridor
- A vehicle is **parked in front of** the building
- Construction **is being carried out** nearby
- Signs are **posted on** the wall

### Cấu trúc hay gặp trong Part 1

| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| S + is/are + V-ing | The workers are loading cargo. |
| S + has been + V3 | The shelf has been cleared. |
| S + is being + V3 | The floor is being cleaned. |
| There is/are + N | There are several boxes on the desk. |
| S + is + adj | The conference room is empty. |

### 10 câu luyện tập Part 1 (T/F)

Bức ảnh: Nhân viên đứng trước bảng trắng, tay chỉ vào biểu đồ, có 5 người ngồi nghe.

Chọn T (True) hoặc F (False):
1. An employee is presenting at the front of the room. (T/F)
2. The audience is standing. (T/F)
3. A chart is displayed on the board. (T/F)
4. The room is empty. (T/F)
5. Someone is pointing at a whiteboard. (T/F)

*Đáp án: 1-T, 2-F, 3-T, 4-F, 5-T*

## Ngày 5 (Thứ Sáu) — Đọc báo cáo tài chính TOEIC

### Đọc và trả lời câu hỏi (Part 7 style)

> **TechViet Corporation — Q1 Financial Summary**
>
> Net revenue for Q1 2026 reached $4.2 million, representing a 22% increase compared to Q1 2025. Operating expenses totaled $2.8 million, resulting in a net profit of $1.4 million.
>
> Cash flow remained positive throughout the quarter, with significant improvements in accounts receivable. The board approved a dividend yield of 3.5% for shareholders.
>
> Looking ahead, management forecasts continued growth in Q2, projecting gross revenue of $5 million. However, increased overhead costs due to the planned acquisition of a competitor may affect the bottom line.
>
> All stakeholders are invited to the quarterly earnings call on May 20th at 10am.

**Câu hỏi:**
1. What was the net revenue in Q1 2026?
2. By how much did revenue increase year-over-year?
3. What is the approved dividend yield?
4. What may affect the Q2 bottom line?
5. When is the earnings call?

*Đáp án: 1-$4.2 million, 2-22%, 3-3.5%, 4-Increased overhead from planned acquisition, 5-May 20th at 10am*
