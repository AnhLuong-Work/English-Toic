## Ngữ pháp — Đảo ngữ & Câu nhượng bộ

### Đảo ngữ (Inversion) — Dùng để nhấn mạnh

Trong TOEIC, đảo ngữ thường gặp trong văn bản trang trọng:

| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| **Not only... but also** | **Not only** did the company restructure, **but** it also downsized significantly. |
| **Rarely / Seldom / Never + trợ từ** | **Rarely** does the company outsource core functions. |
| **Only + adverb/phrase + trợ từ** | **Only** after the mediation **did** they reach an agreement. |
| **Hardly/Scarcely... when** | **Hardly** had the restructuring begun **when** morale dropped. |
| **No sooner... than** | **No sooner** had he joined **than** he received a promotion. |

**Bài tập — Viết lại dùng đảo ngữ:**
1. The company rarely offers sabbaticals. → Rarely ___
2. They had no sooner announced the downsizing than morale fell. → No sooner ___
3. Not only did they cut headcount, but they also reduced salaries. (đã đúng rồi — viết nghĩa)

### Câu nhượng bộ (Concession)

| Từ/Cụm | Ý nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Although / Even though** | Mặc dù | Although morale was low, retention improved. |
| **Despite / In spite of + N/V-ing** | Mặc dù | Despite the burnout, employees stayed motivated. |
| **However / Nevertheless** | Tuy nhiên | Morale fell. However, productivity remained high. |
| **Regardless of** | Bất kể | Regardless of the appraisal results, all staff got raises. |
| **Notwithstanding** | Bất kể (trang trọng) | Notwithstanding the downsizing, the company grew. |

**Bài tập:**
1. ______ the high compensation package, many employees left. (Despite/Although)
2. ______ receiving negative appraisals, she remained committed. (Despite/Although)
3. The company outsourced IT. ______, quality did not suffer. (However/Although)
4. ______ the new code of conduct was strict, compliance improved. (Despite/Although)

*Đáp án: 1-Despite, 2-Despite, 3-However, 4-Although*

## TOEIC Part 2 — Chiến thuật nâng cao

### 6 loại câu hỏi và cách trả lời

| Loại | Ví dụ câu hỏi | Đáp án đúng thường... |
|------|-------------|----------------------|
| **WH-question** | "Who handles the appraisals?" | Tên người / bộ phận |
| **Yes/No question** | "Has the restructuring been finalized?" | Yes/No + thông tin thêm |
| **Or question** | "Should we outsource or hire internally?" | Chọn 1 trong 2 / cả hai / hỏi lại |
| **Gián tiếp/nhờ vả** | "Could you send me the compensation details?" | Đồng ý / giải thích tại sao không |
| **Mô tả** | "What does the new policy look like?" | Mô tả / hướng dẫn |
| **Lý do** | "Why was the headcount reduced?" | Because + lý do |

### Bẫy hay gặp trong Part 2
- **Âm giống nhau, nghĩa khác:** "work" vs "walk", "hire" vs "higher"
- **Từ lặp lại trong đáp án sai:** Câu hỏi hỏi về "retention" → đáp án sai thường có từ "retention"
- **Trả lời gián tiếp:** Đôi khi đáp án đúng không trả lời thẳng câu hỏi

### 15 câu luyện tập Part 2

1. "When does the new flex-time policy take effect?"
   (A) Flexible hours improve morale.  
   (B) Starting next Monday.  
   (C) The HR department manages scheduling.

2. "Has the annual appraisal been completed for all staff?"
   (A) Yes, the results will be shared this Friday.  
   (B) Appraisals are conducted by managers.  
   (C) The staff is very dedicated.

3. "Why was the outsourcing contract terminated?"
   (A) The contract expires next year.  
   (B) Because the quality did not meet our standards.  
   (C) We outsource IT and accounting.

4. "Should the mediation be scheduled for Monday or Tuesday?"
   (A) The mediator is very experienced.  
   (B) Either day works for our team.  
   (C) Mediation helps resolve grievances.

5. "Could someone explain the new code of conduct?"
   (A) The HR director will walk everyone through it at 2pm.  
   (B) The conduct was excellent.  
   (C) We follow company guidelines.

*Đáp án: 1-B, 2-A, 3-B, 4-B, 5-A*
