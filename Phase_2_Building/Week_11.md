## TOEIC Part 7 — Đọc đôi & Bộ 3 văn bản

### Cách tiếp cận Double Passage & Triple Passage

**Double Passage (2 văn bản liên quan):**
- Văn bản 1 và 2 thường là: email + thư / quảng cáo + đánh giá / thông báo + phản hồi
- Một số câu hỏi tổng hợp từ CẢ HAI văn bản
- Chiến thuật: Đọc tất cả câu hỏi trước → câu hỏi nào chỉ cần 1 bài → làm trước

**Triple Passage (3 văn bản):**
- Thường là: email + tài liệu + phản hồi
- Dành khoảng 7–8 phút cho mỗi bộ 3 văn bản

### Luyện tập Double Passage

**Văn bản 1 — Quảng cáo khách sạn:**

> **GRAND PACIFIC HOTEL — SPECIAL SUMMER OFFER**
>
> Book your stay from July 1 to August 31 and receive:
> - Complimentary breakfast for two
> - Free valet parking
> - 20% discount on all excursions booked through our concierge
> - Late checkout until 2pm (standard checkout: 12pm)
>
> Book directly on our website to receive an additional 5% discount.
> Cancellation policy: Free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival.
> Surcharge applies for holiday weekends.

**Văn bản 2 — Email của khách hàng:**

> From: James Park  
> To: reservations@grandpacific.com  
> Subject: Reservation Confirmation
>
> Hello,
>
> I booked a suite for July 15-18 through your website and would like to confirm the following inclusions: complimentary breakfast, valet parking, and the late checkout option.
>
> I also noticed the 20% excursion discount. I'm interested in the city sightseeing tour. Could your concierge provide more details?
>
> Also, I'm a frequent flyer with Pacific Airways. Do you offer any mileage points for hotel stays?
>
> Best regards,  
> James Park

**Câu hỏi:**
1. What is the standard checkout time at Grand Pacific Hotel?
   (A) 11am  (B) 12pm  (C) 1pm  (D) 2pm

2. What additional discount did James receive by booking online?
   (A) 10%  (B) 15%  (C) 5%  (D) 20%

3. What does James want information about?
   (A) Parking rates  (B) Room amenities  (C) A sightseeing tour  (D) Cancellation policy

4. Which benefit mentioned in the advertisement is NOT confirmed in James's email?
   (A) Complimentary breakfast  (B) Valet parking  (C) Late checkout  (D) Excursion discount usage

5. What is James's question that is NOT answered by the advertisement?
   (A) The late checkout time  (B) Mileage points for hotel stays  (C) The cancellation policy  (D) The surcharge for weekends

*Đáp án: 1-B, 2-C, 3-C, 4-D (James mentions but hasn't used), 5-B (mileage points not mentioned in ad)*

## Bài tập tốc độ đọc

**Mục tiêu: đọc đoạn sau trong 2 phút và trả lời câu hỏi**
*(đoạn gồm ~350 từ — nếu hoàn thành trong 2 phút = 175 từ/phút)*

> **MEMO: Travel Policy Update**
>
> Effective July 1, 2026, TechViet Corporation is implementing an updated corporate travel policy. The following guidelines apply to all employees who travel on company business.
>
> **Flight Bookings:** All flights must be booked through the company's approved travel portal at least 14 days in advance to qualify for the discounted corporate tariff. Economy class is required for domestic flights and flights under 5 hours. Business class may be approved for international flights exceeding 8 hours, subject to manager approval.
>
> **Accommodation:** Hotels must be selected from our preferred partner list. The nightly rate limit is $150 for domestic stays and $200 for international stays. Any amount exceeding this limit requires prior approval from the finance department.
>
> **Ground Transportation:** Employees are encouraged to use public transport or the company's approved car rental service. Valet parking and surcharges will not be reimbursed unless pre-approved.
>
> **Cancellations:** If travel plans change, employees must cancel bookings at least 48 hours in advance to avoid cancellation fees. Any fees incurred due to late cancellation without valid reason will be the employee's responsibility.
>
> **Reimbursement:** All travel expenses must be submitted within 10 business days of returning. Receipts are required for all claims over $25. Claims submitted without receipts will not be processed.
>
> For further information, please refer to the full travel policy document on the company intranet, or contact the HR department.

**Câu hỏi (trả lời trong 3 phút):**
1. How far in advance must flights be booked?
2. When is business class permitted?
3. What is the nightly hotel limit for international stays?
4. What happens if an employee cancels late without reason?
5. Within how many days must expenses be submitted?

*Đáp án: 1-14 days, 2-International flights over 8 hours (with approval), 3-$200, 4-Employee pays the fee, 5-10 business days*
