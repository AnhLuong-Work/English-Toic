## Ngữ pháp — Phân Từ (Participles)

### Phân từ hiện tại (V-ing) — Bổ sung cho danh từ
- **The manager reviewing the specifications** found several errors. *(= who was reviewing)*
- **Employees working overtime** will receive additional pay. *(= who work overtime)*

### Phân từ quá khứ (V3) — Bổ sung cho danh từ bị động
- **The product recalled last month** is now back on the market. *(= that was recalled)*
- **Goods damaged during transit** must be reported immediately. *(= that were damaged)*

### Cụm phân từ ở đầu câu
- **Having completed the quality control check**, the batch was approved for shipment.
- **Designed to meet ISO certification requirements**, the new process is highly efficient.
- **Reviewing the lead time**, management decided to change vendors.

### Bài tập — Nối câu dùng phân từ:
1. The subcontractor was hired last year. He manages the procurement process. → The subcontractor ___
2. The goods were damaged during transit. They need to be replaced. → The goods ___
3. After they completed customs clearance, the shipment was delivered. → Having ___
4. The prototype was designed to meet specifications. It passed all tests. → Designed ___

*Đáp án:*
1. The subcontractor hired last year manages the procurement process.
2. The goods damaged during transit need to be replaced.
3. Having completed customs clearance, the shipment was delivered.
4. Designed to meet specifications, the prototype passed all tests.

## TOEIC Part 4 — Chiến thuật

### Các loại bài nói trong Part 4
1. **Thông báo công ty** (announcement)
2. **Quảng cáo sản phẩm/dịch vụ** (advertisement)
3. **Bản tin thời tiết / giao thông** (news/traffic report)
4. **Hướng dẫn sử dụng** (instructions)
5. **Thông báo hành trình** (travel announcement)
6. **Thư thoại** (voicemail)

### Từ khóa nhận biết nhanh
- "This is a message for..." → thư thoại
- "Attention shoppers..." → thông báo cửa hàng
- "Our company is pleased to..." → thông báo công ty
- "Due to..." → lý do thay đổi/hủy
- "For more information, please call/visit..." → kêu gọi hành động

### Bài Part 4 mẫu (đọc to như đang nghe)

> "Attention all staff. This is a reminder that our annual ISO certification audit will take place next Thursday, June 12th. All departments are required to have their quality control documentation ready for review by Wednesday at 5pm. The audit team will visit the manufacturing floor, the warehouse, and the procurement office. Any defects or non-conformances discovered during this period must be reported to the quality manager immediately. Employees who need assistance with their documentation should contact the ISO coordinator, Mr. David Chen, at extension 204. We appreciate your cooperation and look forward to a successful audit."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. What is the purpose of this announcement?
2. When must documentation be ready?
3. Which locations will the audit team visit?
4. Who should employees contact for help?

*Đáp án: 1-To remind staff about the ISO audit, 2-Wednesday at 5pm, 3-Manufacturing floor, warehouse, procurement office, 4-ISO coordinator Mr. David Chen (ext. 204)*
