## Ngày 2–3 — Ôn Ngữ pháp Phase 2

### Checklist ngữ pháp Phase 2

- [ ] So sánh kép: The more..., the better...
- [ ] Quantifiers: many/much, few/little, a few/a little
- [ ] Đảo ngữ: Not only, Rarely, Only after, No sooner...than
- [ ] Câu nhượng bộ: Although, Despite, Notwithstanding
- [ ] Mệnh đề danh từ: that/whether/WH-clause
- [ ] Phân từ: V-ing phrase, V3 phrase, Having V3
- [ ] Gerund sau giới từ: committed to doing, responsible for doing
- [ ] Câu phức với: given that, provided that, whereas, once, by the time

**Bài tập tổng hợp (10 câu):**

1. ______ the downsizing, employee morale remained surprisingly high.
   (A) Despite  (B) Although  (C) However  (D) Therefore

2. Not only ______ the company restructure, but it also relocated its headquarters.
   (A) had  (B) did  (C) was  (D) has

3. We are looking forward to ______ the merger with TechGlobal next quarter.
   (A) complete  (B) completed  (C) completing  (D) have completed

4. ______ the migration is complete, we expect a significant reduction in downtime.
   (A) Before  (B) Once  (C) Despite  (D) So that

5. The more we invest in cybersecurity, ______ our data will be.
   (A) the safest  (B) safer  (C) the safer  (D) most safe

6. ______ filing an affidavit is a legal requirement in this jurisdiction.
   (A) To notarize and  (B) Notarizing and  (C) Notarize and  (D) Have notarized and

7. The patent ______ last year now protects our core technology.
   (A) obtaining  (B) obtained  (C) that obtained  (D) obtains

8. We confirmed ______ the shipment had cleared customs.
   (A) whether  (B) that  (C) which  (D) what

9. ______ receiving a negative appraisal, she continued to perform exceptionally.
   (A) Despite  (B) Although  (C) Even  (D) However

10. The contract is ______ upon approval from the board of directors.
    (A) contingent  (B) dependent  (C) conditional  (D) all of the above

*Đáp án: 1-A, 2-B, 3-C, 4-B, 5-C, 6-B, 7-B, 8-B, 9-A, 10-D*
