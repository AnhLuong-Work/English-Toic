## Ngữ pháp — Gerund Phrases & Infinitive Phrases Nâng Cao

### Gerund làm chủ ngữ (Subject Gerund)
- **Notarizing documents** is required for all legal transactions.
- **Breaching a contract** may result in litigation.
- **Disclosing confidential information** is a serious violation.

### Gerund sau giới từ
- They are responsible **for managing** the trademark portfolio.
- The company is committed **to protecting** intellectual property.
- We succeeded **in ratifying** the agreement without litigation.

**Chú ý:** "committed to", "devoted to", "look forward to", "object to", "accustomed to" — **to** ở đây là GIỚI TỪ → dùng V-ing, không phải to V!

### Infinitive để diễn đạt mục đích
- The legal team was hired **to protect** the company's intellectual property.
- They signed the non-compete agreement **in order to** prevent conflicts.
- The company issued an injunction **so as to** stop the infringement.

### Bài tập:
1. The defendant is accused of ______ (breach) the contract.
2. We look forward to ______ (receive) the notarized documents.
3. ______ (obtain) a patent requires filing a detailed application.
4. The company is committed to ______ (protect) all trademarks.
5. The plaintiff filed a lawsuit ______ (recover) damages.

*Đáp án: 1-breaching, 2-receiving, 3-Obtaining, 4-protecting, 5-to recover*

## TOEIC Part 5 — Luyện tốc độ

### Chiến thuật làm Part 5 nhanh
**Thời gian mục tiêu: 20 giây/câu** (30 câu = 10 phút)

**4 bước nhanh:**
1. Đọc 4 đáp án → xác định loại từ (noun/verb/adj/adv)
2. Đọc câu → xác định vị trí trống
3. Áp dụng quy tắc → loại trừ
4. Chọn đáp án

### 15 câu luyện tập (làm trong 5 phút)

1. The company's ______ policy prohibits sharing client data with third parties.
   (A) confidentiality  (B) confidential  (C) confide  (D) confidentially

2. ______ a patent requires extensive documentation and legal expertise.
   (A) Obtain  (B) Obtaining  (C) Obtained  (D) To obtaining

3. The board is ______ to ratifying the new bylaws at the next meeting.
   (A) agree  (B) agreeing  (C) agreed  (D) agreement

4. The litigation ______ in a verdict in favor of the plaintiff.
   (A) resulted  (B) results  (C) resulting  (D) result

5. The contract includes a force majeure clause ______ protect both parties.
   (A) so as to  (B) despite  (C) although  (D) as a result

6. Both companies signed a non-disclosure agreement ______ protect trade secrets.
   (A) for  (B) so that  (C) in order to  (D) because

7. The ______ of intellectual property is taken very seriously by our legal team.
   (A) protect  (B) protected  (C) protection  (D) protective

8. We are responsible for ______ all trademark registrations are current.
   (A) ensure  (B) ensuring  (C) ensured  (D) to ensure

9. The arbitration clause ______ both parties to resolve disputes without litigation.
   (A) requires  (B) requiring  (C) required  (D) requirement

10. ______ have the new bylaws been rejected so decisively by the board.
    (A) Rarely  (B) Sometimes  (C) Usually  (D) Often

*Đáp án: 1-A, 2-B, 3-C, 4-A, 5-A, 6-C, 7-C, 8-B, 9-A, 10-A*

## Đọc hiểu — Văn bản pháp lý TOEIC

**Đọc và trả lời 5 câu trong 8 phút:**

> **NOTICE OF PATENT INFRINGEMENT**
>
> This letter serves as formal notice that TechViet Corporation has been identified as potentially infringing on Patent No. US11234567, held by InnovateTech Inc. The patent covers the proprietary manufacturing process described in the attached specification.
>
> Under applicable jurisdiction, we are entitled to seek an injunction to prevent further use of this process. However, in lieu of immediate litigation, InnovateTech is prepared to offer a licensing agreement that would allow TechViet to continue operations legally.
>
> Should TechViet choose not to respond within 30 days, InnovateTech will proceed with formal legal proceedings, which may include a claim for damages and attorney's fees.
>
> All communications regarding this matter should be directed to our legal counsel at legal@innovatetech.com.

**Câu hỏi:**
1. Who sent this notice?
2. What is TechViet accused of?
3. What alternative to litigation is offered?
4. What is the deadline for TechViet's response?
5. What may happen if TechViet does not respond?

*Đáp án: 1-InnovateTech Inc., 2-Patent infringement, 3-A licensing agreement, 4-30 days, 5-Formal legal proceedings / damages claim*
