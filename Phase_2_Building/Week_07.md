## Ngữ pháp — Mệnh đề danh từ (Noun Clauses)

Mệnh đề danh từ thay thế cho một danh từ trong câu. Hay gặp trong TOEIC Part 5 & 6.

### Các loại mệnh đề danh từ

| Loại | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|
| **That-clause** | We know **that the campaign was successful**. |
| **Whether/If clause** | We don't know **whether the ROI will improve**. |
| **WH-clause** | Please explain **what the conversion rate means**. |

### Ứng dụng trong câu

| Vị trí | Ví dụ |
|--------|-------|
| Làm chủ ngữ | **That the campaign went viral** surprised everyone. |
| Làm tân ngữ | The manager confirmed **that the KPIs were met**. |
| Làm bổ ngữ | The truth is **that engagement has dropped**. |
| Sau giới từ | We're not sure about **whether to change the positioning**. |

**Bài tập — Nối câu dùng mệnh đề danh từ:**
1. The brand equity improved. We know this. → We know ______
2. Will the niche market strategy work? We're not sure. → We're not sure ______
3. The CEO explained something. The ROI was below expectations. → The CEO explained ______
4. Something surprised the board. The conversion rate dropped by 20%. → ______ surprised the board.

*Đáp án:*
1. We know that the brand equity improved.
2. We're not sure whether the niche market strategy will work.
3. The CEO explained that the ROI was below expectations.
4. That the conversion rate dropped by 20% surprised the board.

## TOEIC Part 3 — Chiến thuật nâng cao

### Dạng hội thoại 3 người (xuất hiện từ 2016)
- Một số bài Part 3 có **3 người nói** thay vì 2
- Chiến thuật: Theo dõi AI sẽ báo: "Questions 32–34 refer to a conversation with 3 speakers"
- Tập trung xác định ai nói gì → không nhầm thông tin giữa các người

### Câu hỏi về Ý định (Intention questions)
Dạng câu hỏi mới: "What does the man mean when he says '...?'"
- Cần hiểu **ngữ cảnh**, không chỉ nghĩa đen
- "I'll see what I can do" = không chắc chắn, sẽ cố gắng
- "That's not exactly what I had in mind" = không đồng ý, muốn thứ khác

### Bài hội thoại mẫu (đọc to như đang nghe)

**Speaker 1 (Sarah):** "I've been reviewing our social media analytics, and the engagement rate has dropped significantly this month."

**Speaker 2 (Tom):** "I noticed that too. I think we need to rethink our content marketing strategy. Our competitors are getting much better conversion rates."

**Speaker 3 (Lisa):** "I've actually been working on an A/B testing plan for our next campaign. We could test two different approaches to see which resonates better with our target demographics."

**Sarah:** "That sounds promising. What's the timeline for this?"

**Lisa:** "I'd say we need at least two weeks to set it up properly. Should I schedule a meeting to go over the details?"

**Tom:** "Absolutely. Let's aim for next Wednesday."

**Câu hỏi:**
1. What problem are the speakers discussing?
   (A) A drop in social media engagement
   (B) A competitor merger
   (C) Budget cuts for marketing
   (D) Staff turnover in the department

2. What does Lisa suggest doing?
   (A) Hiring an influencer
   (B) Conducting A/B testing
   (C) Changing the branding
   (D) Launching a loyalty program

3. When will they next discuss this topic?
   (A) Today  (B) Next Monday  (C) Next Wednesday  (D) In two weeks

*Đáp án: 1-A, 2-B, 3-C*

## Kiểm tra tuần 7

**5 câu Part 5:**
1. We don't know ______ the new branding strategy will increase market share.
   (A) that  (B) whether  (C) what  (D) who

2. The marketing team confirmed ______ the conversion rate had improved by 15%.
   (A) whether  (B) what  (C) that  (D) which

3. Please explain ______ you mean by "niche market penetration" in this context.
   (A) that  (B) what  (C) whether  (D) who

4. ______ the campaign went viral overnight ______ the entire industry.
   (A) That...surprised  (B) What...surprised  (C) Whether...surprised  (D) If...surprised

5. The manager asked us ______ we had completed the A/B testing.
   (A) that  (B) what  (C) whether  (D) which

*Đáp án: 1-B, 2-C, 3-B, 4-A, 5-C*
