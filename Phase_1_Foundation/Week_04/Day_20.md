### B. Ngữ pháp (20 phút) — Từ loại (Word Forms)

**Đây là điểm quan trọng nhất trong TOEIC Part 5!**

#### Nhận biết 4 loại từ
Chiến thuật: **Nhìn vào VỊ TRÍ chỗ trống** → xác định từ loại cần điền

| Vị trí | Từ loại cần | Dấu hiệu nhận biết |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| Trước danh từ | Tính từ | the ___ meeting, a ___ decision |
| Sau động từ to be | Tính từ | is ___, was ___ |
| Sau động từ thường | Trạng từ | works ___, completed ___ |
| Trước trạng từ | Tính từ | ___ important (very important) |
| Làm chủ ngữ/tân ngữ | Danh từ | The ___ is..., We need ___ |
| Làm vị ngữ | Động từ | The team ___ the report |

#### Bảng từ loại hay gặp trong TOEIC

| Danh từ | Động từ | Tính từ | Trạng từ |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| management | manage | managerial | — |
| decision | decide | decisive | decisively |
| efficiency | — | efficient | efficiently |
| success | succeed | successful | successfully |
| approval | approve | approved | — |
| presentation | present | presentable | — |
| agreement | agree | agreeable | — |
| improvement | improve | improved | — |
| employment | employ | employed | — |
| postponement | postpone | — | — |

**Bài tập — Điền từ đúng:**
1. The team made an ______ (agree) to postpone the meeting.
2. She managed the project very ______ (efficient).
3. The chairperson asked for a ______ (decide) from the board.
4. The presentation was ______ (success) — everyone was impressed.
5. The ______ (employ) of 20 new staff starts next month.
6. He gave a very ______ (present) report on the quarterly results.
7. The manager ______ (approval) the agenda before the meeting.
8. We need to ______ (improvement) our meeting procedures.

*Đáp án: 1-agreement, 2-efficiently, 3-decision, 4-successful, 5-employment, 6-presentable, 7-approved, 8-improve*

### C. TOEIC Part 5 — Luyện tập từ loại (20 phút)

**10 câu Part 5 tập trung vào từ loại:**

1. The ______ of the meeting was to discuss the new marketing strategy.
   (A) purpose  (B) purposeful  (C) purposefully  (D) purposing

2. The chairperson ______ the meeting at exactly 9am.
   (A) opened  (B) open  (C) opening  (D) opener

3. All board members reached a ______ on the budget proposal.
   (A) consensus  (B) consensual  (C) consensually  (D) consent

4. The new manager handled the Q&A session very ______.
   (A) professional  (B) professionalize  (C) professionally  (D) professionalism

5. The meeting was ______ due to an unexpected emergency.
   (A) postpone  (B) postponed  (C) postponing  (D) postponement

6. Please distribute the ______ before the presentation begins.
   (A) handle  (B) handout  (C) handling  (D) handled

7. The board's ______ to expand into new markets was unanimous.
   (A) decide  (B) decided  (C) decision  (D) decisive

8. The CEO spoke ______ about the company's future plans.
   (A) enthusiasm  (B) enthusiastic  (C) enthusiastically  (D) enthuse

9. A ______ agenda should be sent to all participants in advance.
   (A) detail  (B) detailing  (C) detailed  (D) detailedly

10. The motion was ______ by a majority vote during the session.
    (A) pass  (B) passed  (C) passing  (D) passage

*Đáp án: 1-A, 2-A, 3-A, 4-C, 5-B, 6-B, 7-C, 8-C, 9-C, 10-B*
