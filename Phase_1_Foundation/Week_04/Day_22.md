### B. Kỹ năng (40 phút) — TOEIC Part 6 & 7

#### PART 6 — Điền từ vào đoạn văn
- 4 đoạn văn × 4 câu hỏi = **16 câu**
- Phải đọc TOÀN BỘ đoạn văn để hiểu ngữ cảnh → chọn từ đúng
- Có 1 câu trong mỗi bài hỏi về **câu hoàn chỉnh** (chọn câu phù hợp)

**Chiến thuật Part 6:**
1. Đọc lướt cả đoạn trước
2. Điền từ theo ngữ cảnh, không chỉ dựa vào ngữ pháp câu đó
3. Câu hỏi chèn câu → đọc câu trước và sau chỗ trống

**Luyện tập Part 6 (đọc và điền):**

> Dear Ms. Thompson,
>
> Thank you for ______ (1) in our media sponsorship program. We are pleased to inform you that our annual advertising campaign will ______ (2) on social media platforms next month.
>
> Our target audience consists of young professionals aged 25–40. We expect to reach over 2 million impressions, with a clickthrough rate of approximately 3%. ______ (3)
>
> If you would like to discuss the sponsorship package in detail, please do not ______ (4) to contact our advertising team at ads@mediaco.com.
>
> Best regards,  
> James Carter  
> Media Director

1. (A) interest  (B) interested  (C) your interest  (D) interesting
2. (A) launch  (B) be launched  (C) have launched  (D) is launching
3. (A) The campaign will include billboard advertising as well.
   (B) Our circulation has decreased significantly this year.
   (C) Please return the signed endorsement form.
   (D) The audience was very large at yesterday's event.
4. (A) hesitate  (B) hesitation  (C) hesitating  (D) hesitated

*Đáp án: 1-C, 2-B (passive voice), 3-A (logical continuation), 4-A*

#### PART 7 — Đọc hiểu
- 15 bài đơn + 2 bài đôi + 3 bài bộ 3 = **54 câu**
- Phần chiếm nhiều thời gian nhất

**Chiến thuật Skimming & Scanning:**

**Skimming** (đọc lướt để hiểu ý chính):
- Đọc tiêu đề, đoạn đầu, đoạn cuối
- Thường 20–30 giây/bài
- Trả lời câu hỏi "What is the purpose of the message?"

**Scanning** (tìm thông tin cụ thể):
- Tìm từ khóa trong câu hỏi → tìm vị trí từ khóa trong bài
- Chỉ đọc kỹ phần chứa thông tin cần tìm
- Trả lời câu hỏi có số, ngày, tên cụ thể

**Luyện tập Part 7:**

> **To:** All Staff  
> **From:** HR Department  
> **Subject:** Environmental Initiative — Office Recycling Program  
> **Date:** May 7, 2026
>
> We are excited to announce the launch of our new office recycling program, effective June 1st. This initiative is part of our commitment to reducing our carbon footprint by 25% by 2028.
>
> Starting next month, recycling bins will be placed throughout the office. Staff are encouraged to separate paper, plastic, and glass. The Environmental Committee will conduct monthly audits to track progress.
>
> All employees who participate actively will receive recognition in our quarterly newsletter. A training session on proper recycling practices will be held on May 20th at 2pm in Meeting Room B.
>
> For questions, contact green@company.com.

**Câu hỏi:**
1. What is the main purpose of this email?
   (A) To announce new environmental regulations
   (B) To inform staff about a recycling program
   (C) To report on carbon emission levels
   (D) To invite staff to an environmental conference

2. When will the program begin?
   (A) May 7th  (B) May 20th  (C) June 1st  (D) 2028

3. What will happen on May 20th?
   (A) Recycling bins will be installed.
   (B) The quarterly newsletter will be published.
   (C) Employees will receive recognition.
   (D) A training session will be held.

4. How can employees get more information?
   (A) By calling the HR department
   (B) By attending the Environmental Committee meeting
   (C) By emailing green@company.com
   (D) By reading the quarterly newsletter

*Đáp án: 1-B, 2-C, 3-D, 4-C*
