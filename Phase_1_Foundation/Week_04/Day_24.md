## PART A — TỪ VỰNG (20 câu)

**Chọn nghĩa đúng:**

1. "outstanding"
   (A) xuất sắc / chưa thanh toán  (B) bình thường  (C) đã thanh toán  (D) quan trọng

2. "mandatory"
   (A) tùy chọn  (B) tạm thời  (C) bắt buộc  (D) hàng tháng

3. "turnover"
   (A) tỷ lệ nghỉ việc  (B) lương  (C) thăng chức  (D) đào tạo

4. "premises"
   (A) lời hứa  (B) cơ sở, mặt bằng  (C) phần thưởng  (D) phí bảo hiểm

5. "inventory"
   (A) doanh thu  (B) hóa đơn  (C) hàng tồn kho  (D) danh sách

**Điền từ đúng vào chỗ trống:**

6. Please keep this information ______. (confidential / confidence / confidently)

7. We need to ______ the costs before signing the contract. (negotiate / negotiation / negotiable)

8. The company has achieved 3 ______ years of growth. (consecutive / consequently / consequence)

9. All employees must attend the ______ safety training. (mandatory / mandate / mandatorily)

10. The ______ results suggest the campaign will be successful. (preliminary / preliminarily / premier)

**Nối từ với nghĩa:**

11. sustainability — (A) tái chế / (B) bền vững / (C) ô nhiễm / (D) quy định

12. reimbursement — (A) bảo hiểm / (B) chi phí / (C) hoàn trả / (D) đặt cọc

13. endorsement — (A) khẩu hiệu / (B) chứng thực / (C) quảng cáo / (D) tài trợ

14. consensus — (A) điều tra / (B) thống kê / (C) đồng thuận / (D) quyết định

15. logistics — (A) hậu cần / (B) giao thông / (C) kho hàng / (D) vận chuyển

**Điền từ vựng phù hợp:**

16. The company will ______ all travel expenses for the conference. (reimburse)

17. The marketing team conducted a customer ______ to understand buying habits. (survey)

18. Sign a 3-year ______ for the office space downtown. (lease)

19. Our annual ______ identifies employees with exceptional performance. (evaluation)

20. The board decided to ______ the vote until more information was available. (postpone)

*Đáp án Part A:*
1-A, 2-C, 3-A, 4-B, 5-C, 6-confidential, 7-negotiate, 8-consecutive, 9-mandatory, 10-preliminary,
11-B, 12-C, 13-B, 14-C, 15-A, 16-reimburse, 17-survey, 18-lease, 19-evaluation, 20-postpone

**Điểm Part A: ___/20**

## PART B — NGỮ PHÁP (15 câu)

1. The report ______ (submit) to the board last Friday.
   (A) submitted  (B) was submitted  (C) has submitted  (D) submits

2. We ______ (not/receive) the payment yet.
   (A) didn't receive  (B) don't receive  (C) haven't received  (D) hadn't received

3. She is the employee ______ handles all client relationships.
   (A) which  (B) where  (C) whose  (D) who

4. If the interest rate ______ (decrease), more people ______ (apply) for mortgages.
   (A) decreases / will apply  (B) decreased / would apply  (C) will decrease / apply  (D) decreases / apply

5. The manager suggested ______ the agenda before the meeting starts.
   (A) to review  (B) reviewing  (C) review  (D) reviewed

6. The new policy ______ (announce) at tomorrow's all-staff meeting.
   (A) will announce  (B) is announced  (C) will be announced  (D) announces

7. ______ the renovation is complete, we can move into the new office.
   (A) Unless  (B) Although  (C) Once  (D) Despite

8. The project was completed ______ — ahead of schedule and under budget.
   (A) successful  (B) successfully  (C) success  (D) succeed

9. The CEO, ______ speech was inspiring, received a standing ovation.
   (A) who  (B) that  (C) whose  (D) which

10. We ______ (plan) to open three new branches by the end of the year.
    (A) are planning  (B) plan  (C) both A and B are correct  (D) have planned

11. All ______ in the survey must be kept confidential.
    (A) inform  (B) information  (C) informative  (D) informed

12. Sales ______ substantially over the past three consecutive quarters.
    (A) have increased  (B) increased  (C) are increasing  (D) will increase

13. Please remember ______ the invoice within 30 days of receipt.
    (A) paying  (B) to pay  (C) paid  (D) pay

14. The contract was terminated ______ non-payment of the outstanding balance.
    (A) despite  (B) although  (C) due to  (D) however

15. ______ the training session is mandatory, attendance will be taken.
    (A) Therefore  (B) Since  (C) However  (D) Nevertheless

*Đáp án Part B:*
1-B, 2-C, 3-D, 4-A, 5-B, 6-C, 7-C, 8-B, 9-C, 10-C, 11-B, 12-A, 13-B, 14-C, 15-B

**Điểm Part B: ___/15**

## PART C — TOEIC PART 5 (15 câu)

1. All candidates are required to submit their ______ before the interview.
   (A) resume  (B) resumes  (C) resuming  (D) resumed

2. The new marketing campaign has ______ more customers than expected.
   (A) attract  (B) attracting  (C) attracted  (D) attractive

3. ______ signing the agreement, both parties must review all clauses carefully.
   (A) Before  (B) Since  (C) Although  (D) Because

4. The environmental committee meets ______ the first Monday of each month.
   (A) in  (B) on  (C) at  (D) by

5. The product launch was ______ postponed due to production delays.
   (A) temporary  (B) temporarily  (C) temporize  (D) temporal

6. Employees who ______ in the recycling program will receive special recognition.
   (A) participate  (B) participating  (C) participated  (D) participation

7. The board has not yet made a ______ on the proposed merger.
   (A) decide  (B) decisive  (C) decision  (D) decisively

8. ______ the preliminary results are positive, the project will proceed to the next phase.
   (A) Despite  (B) Although  (C) Since  (D) However

9. The HR department is responsible for ______ new employees through the onboarding process.
   (A) guide  (B) guided  (C) guiding  (D) guidance

10. The invoice must be paid within 30 days; ______, a penalty will be charged.
    (A) however  (B) moreover  (C) therefore  (D) otherwise

11. All financial reports are reviewed ______ by the external auditors.
    (A) annual  (B) annually  (C) annualize  (D) annualized

12. The company plans ______ its carbon footprint by 30% over the next decade.
    (A) reducing  (B) to reduce  (C) reduce  (D) reduced

13. The new software ______ by the IT department next quarter.
    (A) will install  (B) will be installed  (C) installs  (D) is installing

14. We are looking for a candidate ______ has experience in international logistics.
    (A) which  (B) whose  (C) where  (D) who

15. ______ the comprehensive training program, new employees perform much better.
    (A) Thanks to  (B) Despite  (C) However  (D) Although

*Đáp án Part C:*
1-B, 2-C, 3-A, 4-B, 5-B, 6-A, 7-C, 8-C, 9-C, 10-D, 11-B, 12-B, 13-B, 14-D, 15-A

**Điểm Part C: ___/15**
