## MINI TEST NGỮ PHÁP (30 phút)

### Phần 1 — To be (5 câu)
Điền am / is / are / isn't / aren't:
1. The manager ______ in a meeting right now.
2. We ______ ready for the presentation.
3. ______ you the new applicant?
4. The salary ______ very good. (= not good)
5. I ______ satisfied with my performance.

### Phần 2 — Hiện tại đơn (5 câu)
Chia động từ đúng:
1. The company ______ (hold) annual evaluation in December.
2. She ______ (not/work) overtime on weekends.
3. ______ the HR department ______ (recruit) new staff this month?
4. He ______ (submit) his resignation letter tomorrow.
5. Our team ______ (meet) every Monday morning.

### Phần 3 — Hiện tại tiếp diễn (5 câu)
Chia đúng hoặc chọn thì phù hợp:
1. She ______ (prepare) the budget report at the moment.
2. They ______ (attend) a training session right now.
3. The supervisor ______ (usually/review) reports on Friday.
4. We ______ (interview) candidates all day today.
5. The client ______ (wait) in the lobby right now.

### Phần 4 — Giới từ at/on/in (5 câu)
Điền giới từ:
1. The interview is ______ 10am ______ Monday.
2. She joined the company ______ March 2023.
3. We have team meetings ______ every Wednesday afternoon.
4. The deadline for the financial report is ______ the end of the month.
5. Please arrive ______ the office ______ 8:30am.

## ĐÁP ÁN MINI TEST

**Phần 1:** 1-is, 2-are, 3-Are, 4-isn't, 5-am

**Phần 2:** 1-holds, 2-doesn't work, 3-Does...recruit, 4-submits (hoặc is submitting), 5-meets

**Phần 3:** 1-is preparing, 2-are attending, 3-usually reviews, 4-are interviewing, 5-is waiting

**Phần 4:** 1-at/on, 2-in, 3-on, 4-at, 5-at/at
