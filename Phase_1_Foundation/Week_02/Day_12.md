## PHẦN 1 — ÔN NGỮ PHÁP TUẦN 2 (30 phút)

### Tổng hợp kiến thức

**Danh từ & Mạo từ:**
- Đếm được: a / an / the / số nhiều không mạo từ
- Không đếm được: không dùng a/an, không thêm -s

**Quá khứ đơn:** S + V2/ed | didn't + V | Did + S + V?

**Tương lai:**
- will: quyết định tức thì, dự đoán thuần túy
- going to: kế hoạch đã có, chắc chắn sắp xảy ra
- Present Continuous: lịch cố định đã sắp xếp

**Hiện tại hoàn thành:** have/has + V3 | từ khóa: already, yet, just, ever, never, since, for

**Tính từ vs Trạng từ:**
- Tính từ: trước danh từ / sau be
- Trạng từ: sau động từ / trước tính từ (thêm -ly)

## PHẦN 2 — KIỂM TRA NGỮ PHÁP (30 phút)

### Bài 1 — Chia động từ (5 câu)
1. The company ______ (not/sign) the agreement yet.
2. She ______ (attend) a training session last Friday.
3. We ______ (going to / launch) the new campaign next month.
4. The server ______ (run) more ______ (efficient) since the upgrade.
5. ______ the manager ______ (ever / review) your performance?

### Bài 2 — Tính từ hay Trạng từ? (5 câu)
1. Please respond ______ (quick) to the client's email.
2. This is a ______ (significant) improvement in quality.
3. She speaks English very ______ (fluent).
4. The new software is ______ (considerable) faster than before.
5. The project was completed ______ (successful).

### Bài 3 — Mạo từ (5 câu)
Điền a / an / the / (–):
1. ______ information in the report is confidential.
2. We need to hire ______ experienced IT specialist.
3. ______ server crashed twice this week.
4. Please send me ______ invoice as soon as possible.
5. ______ compliance with regulations is mandatory.

*Đáp án Bài 1: 1-hasn't signed, 2-attended, 3-are going to launch, 4-has run/efficiently, 5-Has...ever reviewed*

*Đáp án Bài 2: 1-quickly, 2-significant, 3-fluently, 4-considerably, 5-successfully*

*Đáp án Bài 3: 1-The, 2-an, 3-The, 4-an, 5-(–)*

## PHẦN 3 — MINI TOEIC PART 5 (30 phút)

**10 câu thực chiến — hãy làm như đang thi thật (không nhìn đáp án trước):**

---

1. The marketing team has ______ a new campaign to promote the brand.
   (A) launch  (B) launched  (C) launching  (D) to launch

2. All employees are required to complete their annual ______ by the end of the month.
   (A) evaluate  (B) evaluating  (C) evaluation  (D) evaluated

3. The client ______ three times since the contract was signed last year.
   (A) visited  (B) has visited  (C) visits  (D) is visiting

4. Please make a hotel ______ at least two weeks before the conference.
   (A) reserve  (B) reserving  (C) reservation  (D) reserved

5. The new database system is ______ efficient than the previous one.
   (A) more  (B) most  (C) much  (D) very

6. Sales revenue ______ by 15% compared to last quarter.
   (A) increase  (B) increasing  (C) has increased  (D) to increase

7. The manager asked all employees to submit their timesheets ______.
   (A) prompt  (B) promptly  (C) promptness  (D) prompted

8. ______ the new compliance policy, all contracts must be approved by the legal team.
   (A) According to  (B) Because  (C) Despite  (D) Although

9. The conference ______ 500 participants from across Asia next year.
   (A) attracts  (B) attracted  (C) is going to attract  (D) has attracted

10. Our IT department ______ the firewall software last Tuesday.
    (A) upgrades  (B) is upgrading  (C) upgraded  (D) has upgraded

---

**ĐÁP ÁN VÀ GIẢI THÍCH:**

1. **(B) launched** — have + V3 (Present Perfect)
2. **(C) evaluation** — cần danh từ sau tính từ sở hữu "their annual ___"
3. **(B) has visited** — "since" + thời gian → Present Perfect
4. **(C) reservation** — cần danh từ sau mạo từ "a"
5. **(A) more** — so sánh hơn với tính từ dài "efficient"
6. **(C) has increased** — hành động có kết quả liên quan đến hiện tại
7. **(B) promptly** — sau động từ "submit" → cần trạng từ
8. **(A) According to** — "Theo [chính sách]" → dùng According to
9. **(C) is going to attract** — kế hoạch trong tương lai với "next year"
10. **(C) upgraded** — "last Tuesday" → quá khứ đơn
