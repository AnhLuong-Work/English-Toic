### B. Ngữ pháp (20 phút) — Danh từ & Mạo từ

#### Danh từ đếm được (Countable Nouns)
- Có thể đếm: 1 employee, 2 meetings, 3 reports
- Có thể dùng **a/an** (số ít) hoặc **the** hoặc để trống (số nhiều)

Ví dụ: **a** meeting, **an** invoice, **the** manager, meetings (nói chung)

#### Danh từ không đếm được (Uncountable Nouns)
- Không thể đếm: information, money, equipment, advice, news, work, staff
- **KHÔNG** dùng a/an, **KHÔNG** thêm -s

Sai: ~~an information~~, ~~two staffs~~
Đúng: **some** information, **the** staff

#### Quy tắc dùng A / AN / THE

| Mạo từ | Dùng khi | Ví dụ |
|--------|---------|-------|
| **A** | Danh từ đếm được số ít, lần đầu nhắc đến, âm phụ âm | a meeting, a report |
| **AN** | Giống A nhưng âm nguyên âm | an email, an invoice, an employee |
| **THE** | Đã nhắc đến trước / chỉ có 1 duy nhất / cụ thể | the manager (của tôi), the report (bạn đã biết) |
| **(không có)** | Danh từ không đếm được nói chung / số nhiều nói chung | money is important, employees need training |

**Bài tập — Điền a / an / the / (–):**
1. She sent ______ email to ______ client.
2. ______ manager of our department is very strict.
3. We need ______ new equipment for ______ assembly line.
4. ______ information in ______ report is accurate.
5. He is ______ experienced employee with 10 years in ______ manufacturing.
6. ______ quality of ______ shipment was excellent.
7. Please give me ______ advice on this project.
8. We have ______ meeting at 3pm. ______ meeting is about the new budget.

*Đáp án: 1-an/the, 2-The, 3-new(–)/the, 4-The/the, 5-an/–, 6-The/the, 7-–(some), 8-a/The*

### C. Kỹ năng — Đọc đoạn văn ngắn (20 phút)

**Đọc và trả lời câu hỏi:**

> TechCorp is a manufacturing company with its headquarters in Hanoi. The company has 500 employees and three branches in Vietnam. TechCorp manufactures electronic components and ships them to clients all over Asia. The quality of their products is excellent, and the delivery is always on time. The management team meets every Monday to review the inventory levels and plan the shipments for the week.

**Câu hỏi:**
1. Where is TechCorp's headquarters?
2. How many employees does TechCorp have?
3. What does TechCorp manufacture?
4. When does the management team meet?
5. What do they review at the meeting?

*Đáp án:*
1. In Hanoi
2. 500 employees
3. Electronic components
4. Every Monday
5. Inventory levels and shipment plans
