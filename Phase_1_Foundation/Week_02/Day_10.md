### B. Ngữ pháp (20 phút) — Thì hiện tại hoàn thành

#### Công thức
```
(+) S + have/has + V3 (past participle)
(-) S + have/has + not + V3
(?) Have/Has + S + V3?
```

| Chủ ngữ | Trợ động từ |
|---------|------------|
| I / You / We / They | **have** |
| He / She / It | **has** |

#### Từ khóa nhận biết
| Từ khóa | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| **already** (đã rồi) | She has **already** signed the agreement. |
| **yet** (chưa — cuối câu) | We haven't received the payment **yet**. |
| **just** (vừa mới) | The client has **just** arrived. |
| **ever** (bao giờ chưa) | Have you **ever** negotiated a contract? |
| **never** (chưa bao giờ) | I have **never** seen such a clause. |
| **since** (từ khi — mốc thời gian) | We have worked together **since** 2020. |
| **for** (trong suốt — khoảng thời gian) | He has worked here **for** 5 years. |

#### Present Perfect vs Simple Past

| Thì | Ý nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|-----|---------|-------|
| **Simple Past** | Thời điểm cụ thể trong quá khứ (biết khi nào) | We **signed** the contract **last Monday**. |
| **Present Perfect** | Thời gian không xác định / ảnh hưởng đến hiện tại | We **have signed** the contract. *(và bây giờ nó có hiệu lực)* |

**Bài tập — Simple Past hay Present Perfect?**
1. We ______ (receive) the shipment yesterday.
2. She ______ (never/negotiate) a contract before.
3. The company ______ (just/terminate) the agreement.
4. ______ you ______ (read) the new compliance policy yet?
5. The dispute ______ (begin) in March 2025.
6. I ______ (work) here for three years.
7. They ______ (already/sign) the amendment.
8. He ______ (not/attend) the meeting last Tuesday.

*Đáp án: 1-received, 2-has never negotiated, 3-has just terminated, 4-Have...read, 5-began, 6-have worked, 7-have already signed, 8-didn't attend*

### C. Kỹ năng — Đọc email thực tế TOEIC (20 phút)

**Đọc email và trả lời câu hỏi:**

> **From:** Sarah Chen  
> **To:** James Park  
> **Subject:** Contract Update  
>
> Dear James,
>
> I hope this email finds you well. I have reviewed the contract you sent last week and I have already made some changes to the payment clause. We have not yet discussed the penalty terms, so I would like to schedule a meeting for this Thursday at 2pm.
>
> Our legal team has identified two clauses that need amendment. They have not caused any disputes yet, but we should resolve them before signing. Could you confirm if Thursday works for you?
>
> Best regards,  
> Sarah

**Câu hỏi:**
1. What has Sarah already done?
2. What hasn't been discussed yet?
3. When does Sarah want to meet?
4. How many clauses need amendment?
5. Why do the clauses need to be changed?

*Đáp án:*
1. She has reviewed the contract and made changes to the payment clause.
2. The penalty terms haven't been discussed yet.
3. Thursday at 2pm
4. Two clauses
5. To avoid potential disputes before signing
