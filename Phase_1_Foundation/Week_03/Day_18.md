## ÔN TẬP NHANH NGỮ PHÁP (30 phút)

### Cấu trúc tổng hợp tuần 3

**Câu bị động:**
- Hiện tại: is/are + V3
- Quá khứ: was/were + V3
- Tương lai: will be + V3
- Hiện tại HT: have/has been + V3

**Mệnh đề quan hệ:**
- who (người) / which (vật, có dấu phẩy) / that (người/vật, không có dấu phẩy) / where (nơi) / whose (sở hữu)

**Điều kiện:**
- Loại 1: If + V (HT), will + V
- Loại 2: If + V (QK), would + V

**Liên từ:** and/but/so/because/although + mệnh đề
**Từ nối:** However, Therefore, Moreover, As a result (+ câu mới)

## MINI TOEIC THỰC CHIẾN (40 phút)

### PART 1 — Mô tả hình ảnh (6 câu)

*Đọc câu mô tả và đánh dấu T (True/Đúng) hoặc F (False/Sai)*

**Hình 1: Nhân viên ngồi tại bàn làm việc, gõ máy tính, có tài liệu xếp bên cạnh**
(A) The man is standing in front of a computer. (T/F)
(B) Some documents are placed on the desk. (T/F)
(C) The employee is typing on a computer. (T/F)
(D) The desk is empty. (T/F)

*Đáp án: A-F, B-T, C-T, D-F*

**Hình 2: Cuộc họp với 4 người ngồi quanh bàn, bảng trắng phía sau có biểu đồ**
(A) Four people are having a meeting. (T/F)
(B) The whiteboard is empty. (T/F)
(C) People are standing around the table. (T/F)
(D) A chart is displayed on the whiteboard. (T/F)

*Đáp án: A-T, B-F, C-F, D-T*

### PART 2 — Hỏi & Đáp (10 câu)

Đọc câu hỏi và chọn câu trả lời phù hợp nhất:

**1.** "Where is the quarterly financial report?"
(A) It was submitted last Tuesday.
(B) In the finance department.
(C) The manager wrote it.

**2.** "Have you processed the insurance claim?"
(A) Yes, it was sent to the insurance company this morning.
(B) The claim was very expensive.
(C) Insurance is important for employees.

**3.** "Why was the maintenance team called?"
(A) They maintain high standards.
(B) Because the air conditioning system broke down.
(C) The maintenance cost is included in the lease.

**4.** "When will the new software be installed?"
(A) It is being installed right now.
(B) The software is very efficient.
(C) By the IT department.

**5.** "Should the meeting be held on Tuesday or Wednesday?"
(A) The conference room is on the 3rd floor.
(B) Wednesday works better for the client.
(C) We already held the meeting yesterday.

**6.** "Who is responsible for tracking the shipment?"
(A) It's tracked by GPS.
(B) The logistics coordinator handles that.
(C) The shipment arrived yesterday.

**7.** "Could you send me the invoice for the freight charge?"
(A) The freight arrived on time.
(B) Of course, I'll email it right away.
(C) The invoice was expensive.

**8.** "Has the deposit been transferred to the new account?"
(A) Yes, it was transferred this morning.
(B) The exchange rate is favorable.
(C) Our bank has many branches.

**9.** "What is the current interest rate for mortgages?"
(A) The interest is paid monthly.
(B) It's 4.5% per year according to the latest announcement.
(C) You need to apply in person.

**10.** "Isn't the lease renewal due next month?"
(A) Yes, we need to sign the new agreement by May 31st.
(B) The tenant moved out last week.
(C) Leasing is cheaper than buying.
