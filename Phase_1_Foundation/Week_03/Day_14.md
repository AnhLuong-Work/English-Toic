### B. Ngữ pháp (20 phút) — Mệnh đề quan hệ

#### Đại từ quan hệ

| Đại từ | Dùng cho | Ví dụ |
|--------|---------|-------|
| **who** | Người | The employee **who** handles refunds is Mary. |
| **which** | Vật / Khái niệm | The receipt, **which** was issued yesterday, is still valid. |
| **that** | Người hoặc Vật (mệnh đề xác định) | The store **that** I visit is downtown. |
| **where** | Địa điểm | The outlet **where** we shop is closing. |
| **whose** | Sở hữu | The customer **whose** receipt was lost asked for help. |

#### Mệnh đề xác định vs Không xác định

**Xác định (không có dấu phẩy):** cung cấp thông tin CẦN THIẾT
- The employee **who** manages the loyalty program is very experienced.

**Không xác định (có dấu phẩy):** cung cấp thông tin BỔ SUNG (dùng who/which, KHÔNG dùng that)
- Mary, **who** has worked here for 10 years, manages the loyalty program.
- The new discount policy, **which** was announced last week, has been well received.

**Bài tập — Điền đại từ quan hệ:**
1. The cashier ______ served me was very helpful.
2. The store ______ we bought the merchandise is now offering a sale.
3. This is the receipt ______ proves your purchase.
4. The customer ______ bag was left at the counter called us.
5. Please contact customer service, ______ is available 24/7.
6. The outlet ______ I work is in the city center.
7. The employee ______ you need to speak to is on leave.
8. This is the discount ______ applies to all loyal customers.

*Đáp án: 1-who, 2-where, 3-that/which, 4-whose, 5-which, 6-where, 7-who, 8-that/which*

### C. Kỹ năng — TOEIC Part 5 với mệnh đề quan hệ (20 phút)

Trong TOEIC, mệnh đề quan hệ xuất hiện nhiều trong Part 5 & Part 7.

**5 câu luyện tập:**

1. Customers ______ spend over $200 receive a 10% discount on their next purchase.
   (A) who  (B) which  (C) whose  (D) where

2. The new loyalty program, ______ was launched last month, has attracted 5,000 members.
   (A) that  (B) who  (C) which  (D) whose

3. Please keep the receipt, ______ will be required for any refund requests.
   (A) who  (B) that  (C) which  (D) where

4. The cashier ______ processed my transaction made an error.
   (A) whose  (B) which  (C) where  (D) who

5. The outlet ______ I bought this merchandise is offering free returns.
   (A) which  (B) where  (C) who  (D) that

*Đáp án: 1-A, 2-C, 3-C, 4-D, 5-B*

*Giải thích:*
- 1: Người (Customers) → who
- 2: Vật với dấu phẩy → which (không dùng that với non-defining clause)
- 3: Vật (receipt) với dấu phẩy → which
- 4: Người (cashier) → who
- 5: Địa điểm (outlet) → where
