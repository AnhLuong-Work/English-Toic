## 30 PHÚT ÔN TẬP (Làm TRƯỚC)

### Ôn mệnh đề quan hệ (15 phút)
Nối 2 câu thành 1 câu dùng đại từ quan hệ:
1. The employee works in accounting. She handles all invoices.
2. The software was installed last week. It has improved our efficiency.
3. The conference center is downtown. We will hold the meeting there.
4. The customer lost his receipt. His purchase cannot be refunded.
5. The cashier made an error. The manager apologized for it.

*Đáp án:*
1. The employee who works in accounting handles all invoices.
2. The software that/which was installed last week has improved our efficiency.
3. The conference center where we will hold the meeting is downtown.
4. The customer whose receipt was lost cannot get a refund.
5. The cashier who/that made an error — the manager apologized for it.

### Ôn từ bán lẻ (15 phút)
Đặt 5 câu có nghĩa dùng từ: retail, discount, refund, receipt, customer service

### B. Ngữ pháp (20 phút) — Câu điều kiện

#### Loại 1 — Có thể xảy ra (Real Condition)
```
If + S + V (hiện tại đơn), S + will + V
```
- **If** the shipment **arrives** on time, we **will meet** the deadline.
- **If** customers **spend** over $200, they **will receive** a discount.
- **If** you **miss** the flight, please contact the logistics team immediately.

#### Loại 2 — Không có thật / Giả định (Unreal Condition)
```
If + S + V (quá khứ đơn), S + would + V
```
- **If** I **were** the manager, I **would change** the delivery policy.
- **If** we **had** more trucks, we **could ship** faster. (could = would be able to)
- **If** the route **were** shorter, we **would save** more fuel.

**Chú ý:** Trong điều kiện loại 2, dùng "were" cho tất cả chủ ngữ (kể cả I/he/she)

#### Trong TOEIC — Dạng hay gặp
- Unless = If...not: **Unless** you book early, you **won't get** a discount.
- Provided that = If: **Provided that** customs clearance is fast, the cargo **will arrive** on time.

**Bài tập:**
1. If the tracking system ______ (work) properly, we ______ (know) where the cargo is.
2. If I ______ (be) in charge, I ______ (redesign) the logistics route.
3. ______ (unless/if) the vessel departs on time, the shipment will be delayed.
4. If you ______ (contact) customer service, they ______ (help) you immediately.
5. The freight ______ (arrive) faster if we ______ (use) air transport.

*Đáp án:*
1. works / will know (loại 1)
2. were / would redesign (loại 2)
3. Unless
4. contact / will help (loại 1)
5. would arrive / used (loại 2)

### C. Giới từ nơi chốn (20 phút)

Thường gặp trong TOEIC Part 1 (mô tả ảnh) và Part 7 (chỉ dẫn đường):

| Giới từ | Ý nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **at** | Tại điểm cụ thể | at the terminal, at the gate |
| **in** | Bên trong không gian | in the warehouse, in the truck |
| **on** | Trên bề mặt | on the shelf, on the desk |
| **near** | Gần | near the exit |
| **between** | Ở giữa 2 vật | between the terminal and the gate |
| **opposite** | Đối diện | opposite the main entrance |
| **along** | Dọc theo | along the route |
| **across from** | Đối diện (Mỹ) | across from the cargo area |
| **next to** | Bên cạnh | next to the customs counter |
| **in front of** | Phía trước | in front of the vessel |

**Bài tập mô tả hình ảnh (TOEIC Part 1 style):**
Mô tả bằng tiếng Anh những gì bạn thấy trong văn phòng/xung quanh bạn bây giờ:
1. My desk is ______ (vị trí).
2. The computer is ______ the desk.
3. There is a [vật] ______ me.
4. The window is ______ the desk.
